# Business Revenue Explanation
## UR Trading Expert LLC

**Date:** January 2025

---

## Business Overview

UR Trading Expert LLC is a Wyoming-based Limited Liability Company that provides professional trading signals and financial education services to individual traders and investors worldwide.

**Business Model:**
- Subscription-based service delivered via automated Telegram bot
- Premium and VIP tier subscriptions
- Direct-to-consumer (B2C) sales model
- Payment processing via Stripe

---

## Revenue Sources

### Primary Revenue Stream: Subscription Services

**Service Offerings:**
1. **Premium Tier Subscription**
   - Monthly subscription for trading signals
   - Access to forex, crypto, and futures signals
   - Educational content and market analysis

2. **VIP Tier Subscription**
   - Enhanced subscription tier with additional features
   - Priority signal delivery
   - Advanced trading strategies and insights

**Payment Method:**
- All subscriptions processed through Stripe payment gateway
- Monthly recurring revenue model
- Direct customer payments to business account

## Revenue Collection Process

1. **Customer subscribes** via Telegram bot or website
2. **Payment processed** through Stripe payment links
3. **Funds deposited** to business bank account (Mercury account once approved)
4. **Service delivered** via automated Telegram bot

**No customer funds are held or managed** - we only receive subscription payments for our services.
