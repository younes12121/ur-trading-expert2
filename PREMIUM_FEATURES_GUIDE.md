**What it detects:** 6 types of divergences between timeframes.

**Types Detected:**
1. **TREND_CONFLICT_BULL_LOWER_BEAR_HIGHER** - Lower TFs bullish, higher TFs bearish

3. **STRENGTH_WEAK_LOWER_STRONG_HIGHER** - Weak momentum in lower TFs
4. **RSI_EXTREME_LOWER_NORMAL_HIGHER** - Extreme RSI in lower TFs
5. **PRICE_RSI_BEARISH_DIVERGENCE** - Price making highs, RSI making lower highs
6. **PRICE_RSI_BULLISH_DIVERGENCE** - Price making lows, RSI making higher lows
