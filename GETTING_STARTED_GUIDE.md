## Understanding Signals

### Signal Components

**1. Asset:** The trading pair (e.g., BTC, EUR/USD)

**2. Direction:** BUY or SELL

**3. Entry Price:** Where to enter the trade

**4. Stop Loss:** Price to exit if trade goes against you
   - **Purpose:** Limit losses
   - **Always use:** Never skip stop loss

**5. Take Profit:** Price to exit for profit
   - **Purpose:** Lock in gains
   - **Strategy:** Can use multiple take profit levels

**6. Confidence Level:** Signal quality (0-100%)
   - 80%+: High confidence
   - 60-79%: Medium confidence
   - <60%: Lower confidence

**7. Analysis:** Why this signal was generated
   - Technical criteria
   - Market conditions
   - AI insights

### Signal Quality

**Ultra A+ Signals:**
- Meet all 20 criteria
- High confidence (80%+)
- Strong market conditions
- Low risk/high reward

**A+ Signals:**
- Meet most criteria
- Good confidence (60-79%)
- Favorable conditions
- Decent risk/reward

**Regular Signals:**
- Meet basic criteria
- Lower confidence (<60%)
- Use with caution
- Smaller position sizes

## Risk Management

### Golden Rules

1. **Never Risk More Than You Can Afford to Lose**
   - Only trade with disposable income
   - Never use money needed for bills/essentials

2. **Use Stop Loss Always**
   - Every trade needs a stop loss
   - Set it before entering
   - Don't move it against you

3. **Position Sizing**
   - Risk 1-2% per trade maximum
   - Example: $10,000 account = $100-200 risk per trade
   - Adjust based on confidence level

4. **Don't Overtrade**
   - Quality over quantity
   - Wait for good setups
   - Avoid revenge trading

5. **Follow Your Plan**
   - Have a trading plan
   - Stick to it
   - Review and adjust regularly

### Risk/Reward Ratio

**Good Trade:**
- Risk: $100
- Reward: $300
- Ratio: 1:3 (excellent)

**Acceptable Trade:**
- Risk: $100
- Reward: $200
- Ratio: 1:2 (good)

**Poor Trade:**
- Risk: $100
- Reward: $100
- Ratio: 1:1 (avoid)
