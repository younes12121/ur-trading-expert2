## ğŸ’¡ Understanding Signals

### Signal Components

Every signal includes:
- **Direction**: BUY or SELL
- **Entry Price**: Where to enter the trade
- **Stop Loss**: Risk management level
- **Take Profit 1 & 2**: Profit targets
- **Confidence**: Signal strength (0-100%)
- **Criteria Passed**: How many filters the signal passed (out of 20)

### Signal Quality

- **A+ Signals**: 18-20 criteria passed (Highest quality)
- **A Signals**: 15-17 criteria passed (High quality)
- **B Signals**: 12-14 criteria passed (Good quality)
- **C Signals**: 8-11 criteria passed (Moderate quality)
