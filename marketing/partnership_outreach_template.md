## Outreach Best Practices

### Do's:
- ✅ Personalize each email
- ✅ Research the influencer's content first
- ✅ Provide clear value proposition
- ✅ Be respectful of their time
- ✅ Offer something valuable upfront
- ✅ Follow up appropriately (max 2 times)

### Don'ts:
- ❌ Mass email blasts
- ❌ Generic templates without personalization
- ❌ Pushy or aggressive language
- ❌ Asking for free promotion without value
- ❌ Spamming or excessive follow-ups

### Tracking:
- Use email tracking tools
- Track open rates and responses
- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor conversion rates by influencer tier
