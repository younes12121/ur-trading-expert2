# Accounting Setup Guide - UR Trading Expert LLC

## Overview

This guide will help you set up accounting for UR Trading Expert LLC using free or low-cost accounting software.

**Recommended Software:**
- **Wave** (Free) - Best for small businesses
- **QuickBooks Online** ($30/month) - More features
- **Zoho Books** ($15/month) - Good middle ground

**This guide uses Wave (free) as the primary example.**

---

## Step 1: Choose Accounting Software

### Option 1: Wave (Recommended for Startups)

**Pros:**
- ✅ Free
- ✅ Easy to use
- ✅ Invoicing included
- ✅ Receipt scanning
- ✅ Bank reconciliation

**Cons:**
- Limited advanced features
- Customer support (paid)

**Best For:** Small businesses, startups, solo entrepreneurs

### Option 2: QuickBooks Online

**Pros:**
- ✅ Comprehensive features
- ✅ Excellent support
- ✅ Integrations
- ✅ Mobile app

**Cons:**
- $30/month
- Can be complex for simple needs

**Best For:** Growing businesses, need advanced features

### Option 3: Zoho Books

**Pros:**
- ✅ Affordable ($15/month)
- ✅ Good features
- ✅ Integrations

**Cons:**
- Less popular than QuickBooks

**Best For:** Mid-size businesses

## Step 2: Set Up Wave Accounting

### 2.1 Create Account

1. **Go to:** https://www.waveapps.com
2. **Click** "Sign Up Free"
3. **Enter** email and password
4. **Verify** email address

### 2.2 Set Up Business Profile

1. **Business Name:** UR Trading Expert LLC
2. **Business Type:** LLC
3. **Industry:** Financial Services / Software
4. **Address:** Your business address (Wyoming registered agent)
5. **Tax ID:** Your EIN
6. **Fiscal Year:** Calendar year (January - December)

### 2.3 Connect Bank Account (After Bank is Ready)

1. **Go to:** Banking → Connect Account
2. **Select** your bank
3. **Enter** credentials
4. **Authorize** connection
5. **Transactions** will sync automatically

**Note:** Wait until bank account is created before connecting.

## Step 4: Set Up Income Tracking

### 4.1 Create Products/Services

**In Wave:**

1. **Go to:** Products & Services
2. **Click** "Add Product/Service"

**Create:**

1. **Premium Subscription**
   - Name: Premium Subscription
   - Price: $39.00
   - Type: Service
   - Income Account: Subscription Revenue - Premium

2. **VIP Subscription**
   - Name: VIP Subscription
   - Price: $129.00
   - Type: Service
   - Income Account: Subscription Revenue - VIP

### 4.2 Record Monthly Revenue

**Manual Entry (if not using invoicing):**

1. **Go to:** Transactions
2. **Click** "Add Transaction"
3. **Date:** End of month
4. **Account:** Cash - Business Checking
5. **Category:** Subscription Revenue - Premium (or VIP)
6. **Amount:** Total for that tier
7. **Description:** "Monthly subscription revenue - [Month]"

**Example:**
- Date: December 31, 2024
- Account: Cash - Business Checking
- Category: Subscription Revenue - Premium
- Amount: $1,950 (50 subscribers × $39)
- Description: "December 2024 Premium subscriptions"

## Step 5: Set Up Expense Tracking

### 5.1 Regular Expenses

**Track these monthly:**

1. **Hosting:** $50/month
   - Account: Software & Hosting → Hosting

2. **Domain:** $2/month (or $24/year)
   - Account: Software & Hosting → Domain Registration

3. **Stripe Fees:** Variable
   - Account: Payment Processing Fees
   - Calculate: (Revenue × 0.029) + ($0.30 × transactions)

4. **Email Service:** $10/month
   - Account: Software & Hosting → Software Subscriptions

5. **Marketing:** Variable
   - Account: Marketing & Advertising

### 5.2 One-Time Expenses

**Track as they occur:**
- Legal fees
- Accounting setup
- Initial marketing
- Business licenses

## Step 6: Bank Reconciliation

### 6.1 Monthly Reconciliation

**Process:**

1. **Go to:** Banking → Reconcile
2. **Select** your bank account
3. **Enter** statement ending balance
4. **Match** transactions:
   - ✅ Check off transactions that match
   - ⚠️ Review unmatched transactions
   - ➕ Add missing transactions
5. **Click** "Finish Reconciliation"

**Frequency:** Monthly (after bank statement arrives)

## Step 7: Financial Reports

### 7.1 Key Reports to Generate

**Monthly Reports:**

1. **Profit & Loss (P&L)**
   - Shows income vs expenses
   - Run monthly to track profitability

2. **Balance Sheet**
   - Shows assets, liabilities, equity
   - Run monthly to track financial position

3. **Cash Flow Statement**
   - Shows cash in/out
   - Run monthly to track cash flow

**Annual Reports:**

1. **Annual P&L**
   - Full year summary
   - For tax preparation

2. **Annual Balance Sheet**
   - Year-end financial position

### 7.2 How to Generate Reports

**In Wave:**

1. **Go to:** Reports
2. **Select** report type
3. **Choose** date range
4. **Click** "Generate Report"
5. **Export** as PDF or Excel

**Check if required:**

**Documents needed:**
- Annual P&L statement
- Annual balance sheet

- Receipts for expenses
- EIN confirmation
- LLC formation documents

**Timeline:**
- January: Gather documents
- February: Prepare tax return
- March: File tax return (or extension)
- April 15: Tax deadline
