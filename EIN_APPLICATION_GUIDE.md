# EIN Application Guide for UR Trading Expert LLC

## Quick Start
- **Website:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- **Available:** Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm Eastern Time
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Time:** 5-10 minutes
- **Result:** Instant EIN (emailed immediately)

## Required Information

Before starting, gather this information:

### Business Information
- **Legal Business Name:** UR Trading Expert LLC (exactly as filed with Wyoming)
- **Business Address:** Your Wyoming LLC registered address
- **Business Structure:** LLC (Limited Liability Company)
- **Date Business Started:** Your LLC formation date
- **Principal Business Activity:** 
  - Select: "Management, Scientific, and Technical Consulting Services"
  - Or: "Other Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services"
- **Products/Services Description:** "Trading signals and financial education services"

### Responsible Party Information
- **Your Full Legal Name:** (as it appears on your SSN)
- **Your Social Security Number (SSN):** Required
- **Your Address:** Can be same as business or your personal address
- **Your Title:** Owner, Managing Member, or Member

## Step-by-Step Application Process

### Step 1: Access the Application
1. Go to: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
2. Click the blue "Apply for an EIN" button
3. **Important:** Complete in ONE session - cannot save and return

### Step 2: Legal Character of Organization
- Select: **"LLC (Limited Liability Company)"**

### Step 3: LLC Classification
- **Most common:** Select **"S"** (Single-member LLC treated as disregarded entity)
- **Alternative:** Select **"C"** if you plan to elect S-Corp status (consult accountant first)

### Step 4: Reason for Applying
- Select: **"Started new business"**

### Step 5: Date Business Started
- Enter: Your LLC formation date (from Wyoming filing)

### Step 6: Principal Business Activity
- Select: **"Management, Scientific, and Technical Consulting Services"**
- Or: **"Other Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services"**

### Step 7: Products/Services Description
- Enter: **"Trading signals and financial education services"**

### Step 8: Responsible Party
- Enter your personal information:
  - Full legal name
  - SSN
  - Address
  - Title (Owner/Managing Member)

### Step 9: Third Party Designee (Optional)
- Leave blank unless someone else is applying for you

### Step 10: Review and Submit
- Review all information carefully
- Submit application
- **EIN is issued immediately!**

## After Receiving Your EIN

### Save Your EIN
- **Format:** XX-XXXXXXX (9 digits with hyphen)
- **Save the confirmation letter** (PDF)
- **Store securely** - you'll need it for everything

### What to Do Next

2. **Update Stripe Account** (requires EIN for business verification)
3. **File Taxes** (use EIN for business tax returns)
4. **Hire Employees** (if needed in future)

## Important Notes

### Daily Limit
- **Only 1 EIN per responsible party per day**
- If you need to apply again, wait until next day

### Application Expiration
- Application expires after **15 minutes of inactivity**
- Must complete in one session

### If Application Fails
- **Try again** (if within daily limit)
- **Call IRS:** 1-800-829-4933 (Mon-Fri 7am-7pm local time)
- **Fax/Mail:** Takes 4-6 weeks (not recommended)

### Common Issues

**Issue: "Application not available"**
- **Solution:** Check time - only available Mon-Fri 7am-10pm ET

**Issue: "Invalid address"**
- **Solution:** Must be physical US address (not PO Box for business address)

**Issue: "Already applied today"**
- **Solution:** Wait until tomorrow (1 EIN per day limit)

**Issue: "SSN required"**
- **Solution:** You must provide your SSN as the responsible party

## Security Reminders

- **Never share your EIN** publicly
- **Only provide to:**
  - Banks (for business accounts)
  - Payment processors (Stripe, etc.)
  - Tax professionals
  - Government agencies
  - Legitimate business partners

- **Beware of scams:**
  - IRS will NEVER call you asking for EIN
  - EIN application is FREE on IRS website
  - Never pay anyone to get an EIN
